The Feed Page
The feed page is the first page you see when you log in. It contains all the posts from the communities you are a part of.

You can like a post by clicking the heart icon, and you can comment on a post by clicking the comment icon. You can also share a post by clicking the share icon.
Posts
You can also navigate through each section of posts: new, which is a time filtered selection of posts, popular, which is a popularity filtered selection of posts, and recommended, which is recommended posts generated especially for you through your preferred categories.
Creating Content
This is also where you will find the create buttons. The top one you can navigate to the create page, where you can create a post or event. The bottom one you can use to create a community.
To see how to do this, navigate to the creating content section.
Recommended Content
The feed page is the first page you see when you log in. It contains all the posts from the communities you are a part of.

You can like a post by clicking the heart icon, and you can comment on a post by clicking the comment icon. You can also share a post by clicking the share icon.
Creating a Post
In the create a post popup that appears, you can select the community you want to post in, the category of your post, and the type of post you want to create. You can also add a title and description to your post, and add an optional image.

You can also tag any content as a Spoiler or Not Safe For Work (NSFW).

If the post cannot be created, this means you are missing some information - so check if you've added both a community and at least one category.
Creating a Community
Creating a community is very much the same as creating a post. You can select the category of your community, and a name, as well as add an image to be used as your community's profile image.

You can select the privacy of your community - public, private, or restricted. You can also tag the community as Not Safe For Work (NSFW).

If the community cannot be created, this means you are missing some information - so check if you've added both a name and at least one category.
Creating an Event
To create an event, simply click the create event button on the feed page. You can then add a title and description to your event, as well as select categories and the community you want to post it in. This community must be one you are a creator of.

This challenge you described is what you want your community followers to do in preparation for the quiz, so be specific and clear.
You can then add tasks for your community members to complete. These tasks can be anything from reading an article to watching a video, and you can add as many as you want. You can also add a due date for the event.

For the quiz you must enter 3 keywords for the AI to generate a relavant quiz, so be sure to use words that are relavant to the challenge you described. Examples would be: "South America", "Amazon River", "Animals".

You can then choose the amount of questions you want your quiz to have. If you cannot create the event, this means you are missing some information - so check if you've added both a title and at least one task.
Navigating
Here you can navigate through your personal posts, any comments you've main on posts, and events you've joined.
Your Information
This is all your personal information. You can see your full name, your username, and your bio. You can also see those who follow you and those you follow. You also have your profile picture and aprofile banner.

In the top right corner you can see your Encompass Points, which corrispond to your leaderboard position.
Editing Your Information
If you click on the pencil icon, you can edit your information. You can change your name, bio, profile picture, and profile banner. Unfortunately, you cannot change your username.
Your Communities
At the bottom of your profile information card, you can see all the communities you are a part of. You can click on any of these to navigate to that community's page.
Searching
On the top of the page is the search bar. Here you can search for any community, user, or post. You can also filter your search by selecting the type of content you want to search for in the navigation bar below the search bar.

In this default posts tab you can see all the posts containing the searched keywords. The related communities tab shows all the communities containing the searched keywords. The related users tab shows all the users containing the searched keywords. By clicking "See More" you can see all the results for that tab.
Communities
Navigating to the communities tab allows you to search through all the communities by searching a keyword or category. You can click on any of these to navigate to that community's page.
People
Here is where you see all the users that match your searched keywords. You can click on any of these to navigate to that user's profile page.
Searching
On the messaging page you will see a list of all your chats to the left. You can click on any of these to navigate to that chat. You can start a new chat by clicking the icon.
Communities
In the chats you can send a new massgae by typing into the bar at the bottom of the page and clicking the icon. You can also send by pressing the enter key.
People
By clicking the notifications button on the menu you can see all your notifications. You can remove any of the individual notifications by clicking the icon. You can also clear all your notifications by clicking the Delete All button at the bottom.
Navigating
You can navigate to the themes page by clicking the More Options button on the menu and then clicking the Themes button.
Change Color Theme
Here you can select the color you want your app to be. You can choose from the default light theme, the dark theme, or the custom themes.
Change Background
You can also select the background you want your app to have. You can choose from the pre-selected backgrounds, or you can upload your own image to use as a background.
Navigating
You can navigate to the themes page by clicking the More Options button on the menu and then clicking the Settings button.
Searching
Here you can navigate through the differrent subsections of your settings. You can change your account information, your profile settings, and your notification settings. Account settings allows you to change your email, password, and filter settings. Profile settings allows you to change your name, bio, profile picture, and profile banner. Notification settings allows you to change your notification preferences.
Communities
Some settings require you to toggle a switch to enable them. You can do this by clicking the switch. Right switch is active and left switch is inactive.
People
To edit a field in the subsections, click the edit icon by the section header.
People
You can then edit the field and click the Save button to save your changes.
Open Event
This card represents an open event. You can see the title of the event, the community it is in, and the due date. You can also see the challenge that has been set for this event. The lock in the corner indicates that the quiz is open and has not been completed yet.
Closed Event
This card represents a closed event. You can see the title of the event, the community it is in, and the due date. You can also see the challenge that has been set for this event. The lock in the corner indicates that the quiz is closed and has been completed. You cannot access the quiz when it is closed. Once you open the quiz, backing out submits your answers.
Challenge Page
This page describes the challenge that has been set for this event. You can see the title of the event, the community it is in, and the due date. You can also see the challenge that has been set for this event. You can also see the tasks that have been set for this event. You can complete these tasks to prepare for the quiz. You can join the community if you are not part of it yet - only members of that community can complete the challenge. At the bottom you can start the quiz.
Answering the Quiz
In the quiz page you can see all the questions, and you can select an answer for each question from the multiple choice selection. Once an answer is selected you cannot change the answer, but you will be able to see if your answer was correct or not. You can see in the upper right corner of each question how many point syou get for answering the questions correctly.
Encompass Points
At the bottom of the quiz page you can see your total points that you have earned for this quiz. You can then submit the quiz and go back to the events page by clicking the Return to Events button.
Leaderboard
Back on the events page is the leaderboard to the right. Here you can see the top 5 users who have earned the most points for events and throughout the site. You can also see your own position on the leaderboard. You can click on any of these to navigate to that user's profile page. Clicking the See More button will take you to the full leaderboard page.
Page Navigation
Similar to your profile you can see all this user's posts and comments on posts that thye have made. On the right is all the user's personal profile information.
The User's Information and How to Follow Them
This card contains all the user's personal information. You can see their full name, username, and bio. You can also see those who follow them and those they follow. You also can see their profile picture and profile banner. You can follow them by clicking the follow button. If you are already following them, you can unfollow them by clicking the unfollow button.
Their Communities
Under the information card you can see all the communities this user is a part of. You can click on any of these to navigate to that community's page.
Community Page
This is the community page, where you can see all the posts in the community. You can like a post by clicking the heart icon, and you can comment on a post by clicking the comment icon. You can also share a post by clicking the share icon. You can also see the community's events.

In the top bar you can see all the community's information, like the name, creator, and description. You can also see the community's profile picture and profile banner. Here you can also join or leave the community.
Closed Event
In the side card you can see more deatils about the community, such as the date it was created, how many points are in the community, what rules are in place for the community, and what ranking (how popular) the community is.
Challenge Page
Under that card you can see all the members of the community. You can click on any of these to navigate to that user's profile page. If you are the creator of that community you have the permission to remove any member from that community.
Answering the Quiz
If you are the creator of the community then you will see this edit icon in the above description bar next to the community name.
Encompass Points
You can click this to edit the community's information. You can change the description, rule set, profile picture, and profile banner.
